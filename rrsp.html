    How Does an RRSP Work?

    Why Invest in an RRSP?

    • Lower your income taxes today by claiming RRSP contributions as deductions
    • Grow your retirement savings faster with tax-deferred investment growth
    • Flexible investment options to match your risk level
    • Use funds for a first home or education (through special government programs)
    • Carry forward unused contribution room to future years
